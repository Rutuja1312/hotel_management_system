Welcome to Luviana Hotel

It is a small comfortable hotel. Our staff offers an attentive high-quality service and is always ready to offer any help to guests. The hotel is arranged on three floors. On the ground floor, apart from the reception, there is a comfortable lounge where you can sit and drink tea or just read. There is also a splendid terrace, where you can relax and immerse yourself into the upcoming morning of a new wonderful day in the atmosphere of Venetian daily life, watch the city bustle & people who are gathering together and whose conversations fill all the streets and alleys. There is an amazing fusion of calm and tranquility at the hotel with hectic and noise outside.
